In late January of 2026, VTuber FioraAlmater played Doom for the first time. What occured were visions of horror, fear and terror not ever before seen by mankind.

It also created a lot of funny sound clips which is the main reason this WAD was made.

When used, this WAD replaces all of the standard Doom Guy sounds and the level start sound with the funniest sound clips Fiora made during her streams. 

Additionally for IWADS and PWADS that use the Doom Guy graphics with their status bar, they are replaced with art of Fiora's Fumo. As Fiora health decreases, her fear increases.

If her health is reduced to 0, the fear overcomes her and she
becomes a pathetic crying mess and you must restart the map.

There are two versions of this WAD available, Modern & Vanilla. 

The modern version is designed to be used with modern source ports such as UZDoom & Woof. 

The vanilla version's graphics and sounds are formatted to be similar to the ones found in the original IWADS and for a vanilla experience.

When using this WAD with other PWADS, it is recommended that FearFumosAndFiora.WAD is added after other used PWADS to ensure the sounds and graphics are not overwritten by the other used
PWADs.
